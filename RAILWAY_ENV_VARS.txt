WHAT EACH VARIABLE DOES:
================================================

DATABASE_URL
  ‚Üí Connects your app to PostgreSQL database
  ‚Üí Railway sets this automatically
  ‚Üí Format: postgresql://user:pass@host:port/db

JWT_SECRET_KEY
  ‚Üí Secures user authentication tokens
  ‚Üí Keep this secret!
  ‚Üí Already generated for you above

PORT
  ‚Üí The port your app listens on
  ‚Üí Railway needs this to route traffic
  ‚Üí Must be 8000

OPENAI_API_KEY (Optional)
  ‚Üí Enables AI bots (Cassie, Sage, Axel, etc.)
  ‚Üí Get from: https://platform.openai.com/api-keys
  ‚Üí Cost: ~$0.01-$0.10 per AI request

STRIPE_SECRET_KEY (Optional)
  ‚Üí Enables subscription payments
  ‚Üí Get from: https://dashboard.stripe.com/apikeys
  ‚Üí Free to use, Stripe takes 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
